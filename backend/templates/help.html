Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open a savings account?

Opening a savings account with Society Bank is simple. Visit our registration page, fill in your details, submit your KYC documents (ID proof and address proof), and your account will be activated within 24 hours. You can also visit our office in person to complete the process.

What documents do I need for a loan application?

For a personal loan, you typically need: (1) Valid ID proof, (2) Address proof, (3) Income proof (salary slip or ITR), (4) Bank statements (last 3-6 months). For education loans, additional documents like admission letter and fee details are required. Our team will guide you on specific requirements.

How long does loan approval take?

Our loan approval process is quick and efficient. Once you submit your application with all required documents, we provide approval within 24-48 hours. Fund disbursement typically happens within 2-3 working days after approval. Express processing is available for urgent requirements.

What are the current interest rates?

Current rates (subject to change): Savings Account - 5% p.a., Fixed Deposit - up to 7% p.a., Personal Loans - 8-12% p.a., Education Loans - 6-9% p.a. Rates may vary based on tenure, amount, and creditworthiness. Contact our team for personalized quotes.

Can I apply for multiple accounts or loans?

Yes, you can maintain multiple savings accounts and invest in different schemes. For loans, you can apply for multiple loans based on your creditworthiness and repayment capacity. Our relationship managers will help you choose the right combination of products for your needs.

How do I track my transaction status?

You can track all your transactions and applications through your member dashboard. Log in to your account, and you'll see real-time status updates for deposits, withdrawals, loan applications, and transfers. SMS and email notifications are automatically sent for all important transactions.

Is my money safe with Society Bank?

Yes, your deposits are fully protected. All deposits up to ₹5 lakhs are insured under the Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation (DICGC) scheme. We also maintain the highest security standards for data protection, including SSL encryption and multi-factor authentication.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email or phone number, and you'll receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password. If you face any issues, contact our support team for immediate assistance.
