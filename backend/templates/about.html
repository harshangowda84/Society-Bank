About Bangalore University Society Bank

Society Bank provides banking services for the university community. We offer savings accounts, fixed deposits, loans, and other financial services tailored to students, staff and alumni. Our mission is to provide secure, transparent and community-focused banking with low fees and responsive support.

Our Vision

To empower our members with reliable financial products and services that support personal growth and community development.

  • Fixed Deposits & Recurring Deposits
  • Personal and Education Loans
